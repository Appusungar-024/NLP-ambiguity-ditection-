## DETECTED AMBIGUITY PATTERNS (5)

1. PP-Attachment
   Pattern: Prepositional phrases attach to different heads
   Example: "I saw the man with the telescope"
   Variations: 2

2. Coordination
   Pattern: Ambiguous scope in coordinate structures
   Example: "Old men and women"
   Variations: 2

3. Relative Clause
   Pattern: Clause attaches to different noun phrases
   Example: "I saw the girl with the telescope"
   Variations: 2

4. Negation Scope
   Pattern: Different scope of negation operator
   Example: "Not all students passed"
   Variations: 2

5. Verb Phrase
   Pattern: VP attaches to different heads
   Example: "She saw the man yesterday walking"
   Variations: 2

## OUTPUT METRICS

1. semantic_ambiguity_score (0.0-1.0)
   - 0.0: Unambiguous
   - 1.0: Highly ambiguous
   - Based on: 1.0 - average_semantic_divergence

2. is_ambiguous (Boolean)
   - True if score >= 0.7 (configurable threshold)

3. num_interpretations (Integer)
   - Count of detected semantic variations
   - Typically 1 (unambiguous) or 2+ (ambiguous)

4. semantic_uncertainty (Float)
   - Entropy-based measure of interpretation diversity
   - Higher = more uncertain

5. primary_meaning (String)
   - Most likely interpretation

6. alternative_meanings (List[String])
   - Other possible interpretations

7. avg_divergence (Float)
   - Average pairwise cosine distance
   - 0.0-1.0 scale
